## About

**SARL-PLUS** — *Smart Analytics & Reinforcement Learning* — is a research team at the **[IDA Lab](https://www.plus.ac.at/aihi/der-fachbereich/ida-lab/)**, part of the **[Faculty of Digital & Analytical Sciences (DAS)](https://www.plus.ac.at/aihi/)** at **[Paris Lodron Universität Salzburg (PLUS)](https://www.plus.ac.at/)**.

We are dedicated to advancing AI through research that spans from theoretical foundations to real-world applications across healthcare, robotics, accelerator physics, and industrial systems.

| Area | Methods |
|------|---------|
| **Reinforcement Learning** | Safe RL, Model-Based RL, Meta-RL, Multi-Agent Systems |
| **Physics-Informed AI** | Hamiltonian Neural Networks, Symplectic Integration, Koopman Operators |
| **Real-World Control** | Particle Accelerators, Industrial Automation, Scientific Facilities |
| **Optimization** | Bayesian Optimization, Gaussian Processes, Regret Theory |

### Principal Investigator

**Simon Hirländer** — *Team Lead & PI, Vice Director of IDA Lab*

PhD in Theoretical Physics (Space Charge Effects in Synchrotrons, 100/100). Pioneer of the first successful Deep Reinforcement Learning experiments on operational particle accelerators at CERN (AWAKE, SPS, LINAC4). Founder of [RL4AA](https://rl4aa.github.io/) and lead organiser of the International RL Bootcamp (2024–2026). 66 publications across 18 research projects.
