Reinforcement learning

Reinforcement learning tries to solve the problem of:
Learning to make good decisions under uncertainty.

Main interests

All activities are focused on real world applications beneficial for local companies.

  • Sample efficient reinforcement learning for continuous control.
  • Safe reinforcement learning.
    • Sample efficient methods which obey some safety constrains.
    • High stake problems.
      • Medical treatments.
      • Study designs.
      • Robotics.
    • Theoretical aspects including explorative aspects.
  • Hard discrete optimization problems.
